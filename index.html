Baby Bear Community

Join our Freshmen/Transfer Retention Program!



Sign Up!


The Office of ASUC Senator Ruchi Shah is piloting the BBC (Baby Bears Community) program to center Freshman & Transfer retention specifically for South Asian, Southwest Asian, and North African students. We're honored to get support from the SSWANA Initiative and the Office of the ASUC Transfer Representative.

With the Fall 2020 semester virtual, we want to create a remote community that will allow for incoming students to connect with peers, make friends, and expand their network. The transition to Cal may be difficult, but we hope you can alleviate some of those challenges by joining the Baby Bears Community!

Please fill out the form so we have a better understanding of your interests and background. The deadline to opt into this program is 9/25.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to rshah@asuc.org or rheamehta@berkeley.edu. Thank you!

South Asian Caucus

Caucus Members



The caucus is comprised of the leadership of the South Asian identifying organizations at UC Berkeley. Each group adds their own virtue to the diversity of the community and we aspire to use this group as a way to have unity and collaboration with organizations. The Caucus is chaired by the South Asian Community Endorsed ASUC Senator (Ruchi Shah 2020-2021).

Caucus Constitution

The constitution outlines the core values each org in the caucus aims to uphold. The document also explains responsibilities for Cacus Chair & process for endorsement for ASUC senate.
