Our ASUC Senate Office

The South Asian Community Endorsed Senator's Office.


Office Goals


,

The 2020-2021 South Asian Commmunity Endorsed ASUC Senator is Ruchi Shah. ASUC Shah's office plans to advocate for South Asian students through providing resources, representation, and spaces for conversations. As an essential goal, the office aims to unite the community and use our privilege to uplift other marginalized communities on campus.

Additionally, ASUC Shah will also be working on STEM and Career Support initiatives.
